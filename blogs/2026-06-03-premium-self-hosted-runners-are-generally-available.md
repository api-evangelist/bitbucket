Atlassian has launched Premium Runners for Bitbucket as a generally available feature, maintaining a free tier of up to 100 self-hosted runners after user feedback. Organizations needing greater scale or advanced management can upgrade to Premium Runners at $15 per month per concurrent build slot, which include custom storage and queue management capabilities. The free tier ensures smaller teams can continue using self-hosted runners without cost while larger enterprises gain a supported upgrade path.
